Scratch?

Scratch is the world's largest children's coding community, a coding language with a simple visual interface that young people use to create digital stories, games, and animations Scratch was designed by the nonprofit Scratch Foundation, Scratch is designed, developed, and maintained by the nonprofit Scratch Foundation. Scratch promotes computational thinking and problem-solving skills, creative teaching and learning, self-expression and collaboration, and equity in computer use. Scratch is completely free and available in over 70 languages.
